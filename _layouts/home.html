Open Source


For me open source work is extremely important for society, and as a software engineer, is the least I can to do help society. I have been working in open-source software for more than 3 years and I strongly believe that the most vital infrastructure of our society should be open-source for more reasons that I could count. In my free time I also volunteer as a staff member for OpenMined Research team which enables researchers across multiple universities to develop open-source research.
